Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you register

Who is this conference for?

This event is designed for engineers, founders, and builders at every level — from students writing their first lines of code to senior developers scaling production systems. Whether you're here to learn, network, or launch your next big idea, there's a track for you.

What's included in my ticket?

All tickets include access to keynote talks and the main conference floor. Higher tiers add hands-on workshops, 1-on-1 mentorship sessions, VIP networking events, and recorded session access so you never miss a talk.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes. Standard and Team ticket holders get access to recordings within 7 days of the event. Team tier also includes early access to speaker slide decks and workshop materials.

Can I get a refund or transfer my ticket?

Tickets are fully refundable up to 30 days before the event, and transferable to a colleague at any time — just reach out to our support team with your order number.

Is there a discount for students or teams?

Yes — students get 50% off Standard tickets with a valid .edu email, and teams of 5 or more save 15% automatically at checkout. Contact us for larger group rates.
